Our Nail Services

Services Offered

1. Gel Nail Extensions Soft, lightweight, and glossy nail extensions that are long-lasting and elegant. Perfect for everyday wear or special occasions.

Gel nail extensions are artificial nails made using a special gel formula that helps create longer, stronger, and more beautiful nails. The gel is applied over a nail form or tip, shaped to the desired length, and then cured under a UV or LED lamp to harden it. These extensions feel lighter and more natural compared to acrylic nails, which makes them a popular choice. They also provide a glossy, smooth finish that can be customized with different colors, shapes, and designs. Gel extensions can last for weeks with proper care and are known to be less damaging to natural nails. Overall, they are a great option for anyone who wants durable, stylish, and natural-looking nails.

2. Acrylic Nail Extensions Durable and versatile nails ideal for longer lengths and bold designs. Choose from classic, chic, or creative styles.

Acrylic nail extensions are artificial nails made by combining a liquid monomer and a powder polymer to create a hard protective layer over the natural nail. They are used to add length, strength, and shape to the nails. The mixture quickly hardens when exposed to air, forming a solid extension. Acrylics are known for their durability and ability to last for weeks. They can be customized with different shapes and designs. They are commonly used in salons as a reliable method for enhancing nail appearance. Acrylic nail extensions also provide a firm surface that supports nail art. Even though they are strong, they should be applied and removed properly to protect the natural nails. Overall, acrylic nail extensions are a widely used technique for creating long-lasting artificial nails.

3. Nail Art & Custom Designs Personalized nail art to match your mood, outfit, or personality. From minimalistic designs to glittery, kawaii, or trendy patterns.

Nail Art & Custom Designs refer to creative and personalized decorations applied to the nails to enhance their appearance. This can include painted patterns, intricate drawings, glitter, gems, stickers, and other decorative elements. The designs are usually created using nail polish, gel, acrylic, or specialized tools for detailing. Nail art allows individuals to express their personality, style, and creativity through their nails. It can range from simple, minimal looks to complex and artistic designs. Custom designs are made specifically based on a client’s preference, theme, or occasion. They make the nails stand out and add a unique, fashionable touch. Overall, Nail Art & Custom Designs turn ordinary nails into stylish and artistic expressions.

4. French Tip Nails Elegant and timeless French manicure extensions for a clean, sophisticated, and classy look.

French Tip Nails are a classic nail style where the tips of the nails are painted white while the rest of the nail remains a natural, nude, or light pink color. This design creates a clean and elegant look that never goes out of style. It is commonly done using polish, gel, or acrylic. French tips can be worn on natural nails or extensions. They are popular because they look neat, simple, and match any outfit or occasion. Modern versions may use different colors or shapes, but the traditional white tip remains the most recognizable. Overall, French Tip Nails are known for their timeless beauty and sophisticated appearance.

5. Nail Repair & Maintenance Care for damaged nails, reshape, or refresh your extensions. Includes filing, buffing, and polish touch-ups.

Nail Repair & Maintenance refers to the process of fixing damaged nails and keeping them healthy, clean, and strong. It includes repairing cracks, breaks, lifting, or weak spots using gel, acrylic, or special treatments. Maintenance also involves regular filing, cleaning, and retouching to keep the nails looking neat and in good condition. These services help prevent further damage and extend the life of nail extensions or natural nails. Proper nail repair restores the nail’s appearance and strength. Regular maintenance ensures that nails stay healthy, durable, and well-groomed. Overall, Nail Repair & Maintenance focuses on protecting, fixing, and caring for the nails to keep them looking their best.
